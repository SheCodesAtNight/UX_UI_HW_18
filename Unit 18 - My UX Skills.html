My UX Skills

User Research

My approach to User Research is easy. I keep things simple and to the point. I generate ideas of a possible solution, & then I create a survey and interview individuals to learn more about the problem at a consumer level. From there, I take the results of the survey and (using Miro) I sort the information to find a commonality amongst the details. Miro will display possible solutions to usability issues, which will be used on behalf of the client.

Front-End Web Developer

I use HTML, CSS, Javascript, JQuery, & Bootstrap to create websites for clients once I’ve received all data from User Research.

Communication

I’m confident with communicating a technical idea during a presentation to a client or team. I have the ability to break down complicated content on a level of understanding, that makes learning about technical topics easier for people to comprehend.
